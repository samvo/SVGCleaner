=== Remove subsequent segments command from paths

If path segment has the same type as previous - we can skip command specifier.

////
<svg>
  <path d="M 10 10 L 90 10 L 90 90
           L 10 90 L 10 10 z"
        fill="none" stroke="red"
        stroke-width="2"/>
</svg>
SPLIT
<svg>
  <path d="M 10 10 L 90 10 90 90
           10 90 10 10 z"
        fill="none" stroke="red"
        stroke-width="2"/>
</svg>
////
